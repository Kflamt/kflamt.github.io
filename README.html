Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 24, 2025

Padel Score Tracker does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app functions entirely on your device and does not transmit data to any server.

Data Storage

All scoring data is stored locally on your smartwatch. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted permanently.

Permissions

Padel Score Tracker does not request or use any device permissions beyond standard storage for saving match progress.

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page.
