Privacy Policy for Padel Score Tracker

Effective Date: June 24, 2025

Padel Score Tracker ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App on your Wear OS smartwatch.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or share any personal data.

The App:

  • Does not require an internet connection
  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not track your location or device activity
  • Does not store data outside your device

2. Local Storage

All scoring data, match history, and preferences are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted anywhere and is only accessible by you.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties.

4. Analytics & Ads

This App does not use analytics tools or display advertisements.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App does not target or collect data from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if features change. You will be notified of significant changes through the app or app store listing.
